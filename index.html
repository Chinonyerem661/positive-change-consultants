FAQ

Common questions.

Who do you consult for?

We support medical practices, outpatient clinics, wellness providers, and healthcare administrators who want clearer systems and measurable improvement.

Can I request a virtual appointment?

Yes. Add your preference in the appointment notes and the team can confirm the meeting format during follow-up.

What happens after I submit an appointment?

Your request appears on the calendar immediately. A real production site would connect this form to email, CRM, or scheduling software.

Do you handle urgent medical issues?

No. This site is for consultancy appointments only. Patients with urgent symptoms should contact emergency services or a licensed clinician directly.
